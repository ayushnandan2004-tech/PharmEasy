Project Description

This comprehensive analysis examines PharmEasy's customer reviews to identify critical pain points in healthcare service delivery, assess customer satisfaction levels, and provide evidence-based recommendations for service improvement and enhanced patient experience. The study employs mixed-methods research approach combining quantitative metrics and qualitative insights from multiple review platforms.
